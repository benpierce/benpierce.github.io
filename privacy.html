Privacy Policy for User Activity Dashboard

This privacy policy applies to the User Story Dashboard Jira App (the "App") and describes how we collect, use, and share personal information when you use the App.

 

Collection of Personal Information

The App runs exclusively within Atlassian's cloud boundaries and does not communicate with or store data in any third-party system. The App only processes data that is already stored on Atlassian's servers and is necessary for the proper functioning of the App.

The App's only persistence capabilities interface with Atlassian's storage system in order to save and load user's filter preferences.

 

Log Data

In the event of an error or significant event (such as a new installation or uninstall), the App may receive some informational log data that can then be used by DevJitsu to troubleshoot problems or monitor the App.

Log information collected may include:

  • Site host
  • Error or event messages
  • Dates and times
  • Invocation success rate
  • Invocation failure rate

 

Use of Personal Information

Any personal information that is processed by the App is only used to provide the functionality of the App. We do not have access to customer data through this App and as such, we do not (and cannot) share or disclose any personal information to third parties.

 

Data Retention

The App only processes data that is already stored on Atlassian's servers and does not store any personal information.

 

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the App, please contact us at privacy@devjitsu.ca

 

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any update to this privacy policy will be highlighted in our release notes.
